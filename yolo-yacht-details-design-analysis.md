## Design Elements

### Colors
- **Dark blue**: #1e3a8a (headings, text)
- **Yellow/beige**: Curved background wave
- **White**: Main background
- **Light gray**: Section backgrounds

### Typography
- **Yacht name**: Very large, bold, uppercase
- **Section headings**: Medium, bold, dark blue
- **Labels**: Uppercase, smaller, dark blue
- **Values**: Regular weight, dark gray

### Icons
- Simple line icons for quick specs
- Pin icon for location

### Spacing
- Clean, generous white space
- Clear section separation
- Grid-based layout
